Services
At Chillica, we offer a flavorful experience with dine-in, takeaway, home delivery, catering, and more — all served with spice and style!

Dine-In Service

Customers are welcomed to sit and enjoy meals in a comfortable environment. The ambiance, seating, music, and table service enhance the dining experience.

Takeaway/Carry-Out

Customers can order food and take it with them to eat elsewhere. This service is ideal for those who don’t have time to dine in. and many more for you

Home Delivery

Food is delivered to the customer’s home or office by the restaurant or through a delivery partner.It adds convenience for people who prefer eating at home.

Online Ordering

Customers can place orders through the restaurant’s website or app. Menus, prices, and special deals are displayed for easy selection.and many more for you

Buffet Service

A self-service style where customers serve themselves from a variety of dishes. It’s often used during lunch hours, parties, or special events. Buffets allow customers to try multiple items at a fixed price.

Catering for Events

The restaurant provides food and drinks for events like weddings, birthdays, or corporate meetings.Menus are often customized based on event needs and guest preferences and many more for you

Reservation Booking

Customers can reserve tables in advance, especially for busy hours or occasions. It ensures a guaranteed spot and reduces waiting time.

Live Cooking

Food is prepared in front of customers, often at a live grill or cooking counter. It adds entertainment and transparency to the dining experience.and many more for you
